What is PhishRadar?

Traditional phishing detectors give you a simple "safe" or "dangerous" verdict. PhishRadar goes further — it explains why a URL is dangerous using AI, making threat intelligence accessible to non-technical teams, junior SOC analysts, and anyone who wants to understand phishing attacks.

The platform aggregates threat data from multiple real-time feeds (URLhaus, OpenPhish, VirusTotal, and more), uses a machine learning classifier trained on 650K+ URLs, and generates human-readable explanations via Claude AI.

How It Works

1
Threat Feed Check
URL is checked against live threat feeds (URLhaus, OpenPhish, VirusTotal, Google Safe Browsing) for instant verdicts.
2
Feature Extraction & ML Classification
14+ features extracted (domain age, SSL validity, typosquatting, redirect chains). Gradient Boosting classifier trained on 650K+ URLs delivers the verdict.
3
AI-Powered Explanation
Claude AI generates a plain-English explanation of the threat, including risk factors, severity level, and recommended action.
